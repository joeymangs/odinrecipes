Joe's Tortellini

Description

I originally concocted this meal when making bulk meal-preps to help me gain weight. As it is very high in fat and protein, it has been a staple meal for me for years when I'm trying to put on weight. If you are on a diet or trying to lose weight, it's best to avoid even looking at this meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Family-Size bags of Four Cheese Tortellini
  • 10-15 Spicy Italian Bratwursts
  • Sauteed Mushrooms
  • Sauteed Onions
  • Sauteed Peppers
  • Garlic & Herb Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Slice each brat into 5 or 6 pieces
  2. Begin cooking brats in a pan on low heat for about 30 minutes
  3. Cut mushrooms, onions, and peppers
  4. Place the veggies into a separate pan on medium heat
  5. Begin boiling 40 oz of water
  6. Cut open bags of tortellini and place in boiling water for 3-4 minutes, stir gingerly
  7. Empty out brats and veggies into strainer, place back into pan when finished
  8. Empty tortellini into strainer
  9. Place a bottom layer tortellini into generously sized bowl
  10. Place another layer of brats and veggies on top of that
  11. Smother in tomato sauce
  12. Repeat steaps 9, 10, & 11 until complete
  13. Enjoy!
