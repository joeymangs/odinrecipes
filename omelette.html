Omelette

Description

My favorite savory breakfast meal for any meal of the day. Packed with fat and protein to keep me energized on a busy day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Mushroom
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Ham
  • Turkey
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Crack 6 eggs into bowl
  2. Add 4-6oz of milk
  3. Add a splash of water
  4. Whip egg yolk and milk into a fine thick liquid
  5. Place sliced veggies and strips of meat into pan, cook on medium/low
  6. Pour egg yolk liquid into pan evenly, cook on medium
  7. Press angled wooden spatula into all sides of egg to compress together
  8. Empty all veggies/meat into one side of omelette
  9. Add your cheese of choice
  10. Fold empty side of egg over and on top of toppings
  11. Place lid over the top of the pan to cook thoroughly
  12. Place omelette on plate when finished and add avocado slices
  13. Voila!
